Weekly Specials Calendar

Something special on the menu every single day of the week. No exceptions, no fine print.

Monday
Blue Plate Special

A rotating comfort food classic — Bobby Ray picks something different each Monday. Past specials include meatloaf with mashed potatoes, chicken and dumplings, and beef stew. Full plate with two sides.

$12
Tuesday
Southern Taco Tuesday

Our Copper Skillet twist on Taco Tuesday — pulled pork with vinegar slaw in a cast-iron-toasted tortilla, or catfish bites with remoulade and pickled jalapeños. Two tacos with sweet tea included.

$11
Wednesday
Breakfast for Dinner

All of our breakfast items are available at dinner prices. Full Country Breakfast for $9, Biscuits & Gravy for $7, and the Pancake Stack with a free side of bacon for $11.

From $7
Thursday
Locals Night

Show your Pulaski County ID and receive 10% off your entire check. Our way of saying thank you to the community that built this place. Applies to dine-in only, cannot be combined with other offers.

10% Off
Friday
Friday Fish Fry

An all-you-can-eat catfish fry starting at 5pm. Cornmeal-crusted fillets, hush puppies, coleslaw, and sweet tea. This is a Copper Skillet tradition going back to our very first year.

$16
Saturday
Saturday Brunch

Extended breakfast service 9am–2pm with brunch-exclusive specials: eggs benedict with country ham, French toast with fresh berry compote, and bottomless coffee.

9am – 2pm
Sunday
Pot Roast Sunday

Bobby Ray's signature slow-braised chuck roast available all day Sunday only. Six-hour braise with root vegetables in a rich red wine jus. Served with mashed potatoes and green beans. Sells out by 3pm.

$19

Loretta Mae bakes something new every single day. Could be a blackberry skillet cake, a chess pie, a bread pudding with whiskey sauce, or something we haven't put on the permanent menu yet.
