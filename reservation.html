Walk-In Policy

Walk-ins are always welcome at The Copper Skillet. We reserve a portion of our seating for walk-in guests every day. Wait times are typically 10–20 minutes during peak hours (7–9am, 12–1pm, 6–7:30pm). For groups of 6 or more, reservations are strongly recommended.
